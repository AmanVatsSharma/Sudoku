Bundled sound effects are from Kenney "Interface Sounds" (CC0 1.0 Universal).
Source: https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds
Pack mirror used for bundling: https://github.com/Calinou/kenney-interface-sounds

Files use softer glass / air-style sources, lightly processed (high/low pass + level) for a calmer UI:

- tap.wav   ← glass_001.wav
- place.wav ← glass_003.wav
- error.wav ← minimize_002.wav (soft downshift, not a sharp error beep)
- hint.wav  ← open_002.wav
- win.wav   ← glass_005.wav
